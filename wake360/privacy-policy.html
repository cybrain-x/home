Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026  ·  cybrainx.com

This Privacy Policy applies to the Wake360 mobile application ("App"), developed by Cybrainx. It explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Usage & Analytics: Device info, OS version, interactions, crash reports — via Firebase/Google Analytics.
  • Alarm Data: Your alarm schedules and challenge preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not transmit this data to our servers.
  • Advertising Data: Google AdMob may collect identifiers for ad delivery.
  • Purchase Data: Handled by Google Play/Apple App Store. We do not store payment details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate alarms and brain challenges correctly;
  • To serve ads via Google AdMob (free tier);
  • To analyze usage and fix bugs via Firebase;
  • To manage subscriptions via RevenueCat.

3. Permissions Required

  • Alarm & Notifications: Required to trigger alarms at scheduled times.
  • Do Not Disturb access: Optional, used to ensure alarms bypass DND mode.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob — advertising
  • Firebase / Google Analytics — analytics
  • RevenueCat — subscriptions

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13.

6. Data Security

Your alarm data stays on your device. We apply reasonable measures for analytics data.

7. Your Rights

Contact owner@cybrainx.com to exercise data rights.

8. Changes

We may update this policy at any time. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
