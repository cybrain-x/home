Privacy Policy

Inspire Me Now

This Privacy Policy explains how Inspire Me Now collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the Inspire Me Now mobile apps for Android and iOS and related support pages hosted by Cybrainx.

Last updated: April 9, 2026

1. Scope 2. Information We Collect 3. How We Use Information 4. Sharing and Service Providers 5. Ads and Analytics 6. Retention and Deletion 7. Your Choices and Rights 8. Security 9. Children 10. International Transfers 11. Changes 12. Contact

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the Inspire Me Now mobile applications, including free and premium features, guest usage, sign-in flows, and related support pages linked from the app. It covers information handled by Cybrainx and by the third-party service providers that help us operate the app.

The app is designed as a spiritual companion. It is not presented as a medical diagnosis or emergency service. If you use diary or reflection features, please avoid sharing more personal information than you are comfortable storing in a cloud account.

2. Information We Collect

Account and sign-in data When you create an account or sign in, we may collect your email address, display name, Firebase user ID, authentication provider, and provider-specific identifiers needed to complete sign-in through email/password, Google, Apple, or Facebook.
Profile and preferences We may store profile settings such as gender, religion, reminder preferences, notification time, notification frequency, language preferences, and similar account settings. Current mobile builds do not require precise GPS permission.
Emotional and spiritual content We may store the feelings you select, mood or intensity entries, reflections you save, favorites, diary or journal entries, tags, prompts used, and related timestamps. If you choose to write in the diary, the text you enter is stored in your account.
Subscription and billing data If you purchase premium access, we receive subscription status, entitlement status, product identifiers, store transaction status, and app user identifiers through the app stores and RevenueCat. We do not receive your full credit card number.
Advertising and device data If ads are enabled, Google Mobile Ads and its partners may process device identifiers, app and device information, approximate network information, and ad performance data. Current app code requests non-personalized ads where supported, but ad providers may still process technical data needed to serve and measure ads.
Local files and on-device storage We store app settings, temporary share media, guest favorites, guest check-ins, downloaded ambient media, and reminder settings on your device to support app functionality. Some of this information may stay only on your device unless you sign in or explicitly share content.

Information you provide directly

  • Email address, password, or sign-in choice.
  • Profile selections such as gender, religion, and reminder preferences.
  • Feelings, ratings, favorites, diary entries, and other content you choose to save.
  • Support requests you submit through our contact channels.

Information collected automatically

  • Technical app and device information needed for authentication, subscriptions, notifications, and ads.
  • Temporary file paths and cache files created when you generate shareable images or videos.
  • Subscription state and entitlement information returned by RevenueCat and the app stores.

Information from third parties

  • Sign-in providers such as Google, Apple, and Facebook may provide account identifiers, email address, and basic profile information allowed by your provider settings.
  • Apple App Store, Google Play, and RevenueCat may provide purchase and entitlement status.
  • Google Mobile Ads may provide ad response and ad measurement data.

3. How We Use Information

  • To create and secure your account and let you sign in.
  • To personalize biblical passages, reflections, reminder copy, and user experience.
  • To save your favorites, check-in history, and diary content across sessions and devices.
  • To deliver local reminders and manage your notification preferences.
  • To provide premium subscriptions, restore purchases, and manage billing status.
  • To show ads in supported versions of the app.
  • To create shareable media only when you request that feature.
  • To maintain, troubleshoot, secure, and improve the app.
  • To comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests.

4. Sharing and Service Providers

We do not sell your personal information in the ordinary sense of selling customer lists for cash. We do share information with the service providers and platforms needed to run the app:

  • Firebase for authentication, cloud database, and related backend services.
  • Google Sign-In, Apple Sign-In, and Facebook Login when you choose those sign-in methods.
  • Google Mobile Ads / AdMob for ads and related ad measurement.
  • RevenueCat for subscription and entitlement management.
  • Apple App Store and Google Play for in-app purchase processing.
  • GitHub-hosted media sources when the app refreshes optional ambient images or sounds.
  • Device-level sharing and text-to-speech services when you use sharing or accessibility features.

We may also disclose information if required by law, to protect users, to enforce our terms, or in connection with a business transfer such as a merger, acquisition, or asset sale.

5. Ads and Analytics

Inspire Me Now may display ads in some versions or subscription tiers. Our current mobile code requests non-personalized ads where supported. Even so, advertising providers may still process device, identifier, and ad response data to deliver ads, limit fraud, and report ad performance.

If we later enable personalized ads, tracking, remarketing, or new analytics tools, we will update this Privacy Policy and any required in-app notices or store disclosures before shipping those changes.

6. Retention and Deletion

We keep personal information for as long as it is reasonably needed to operate the app, maintain your account, provide premium features, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and meet legal requirements.

  • Account, favorites, check-ins, and diary content are generally kept until you delete them or delete your account.
  • Guest-only information stored locally on your device may remain until you remove the app, clear app data, or sign in and replace local state.
  • Temporary share files and cached media may remain on-device for a limited period until they are pruned or removed by the operating system or the app.
  • Backups, logs, and fraud-prevention records may remain for a limited time where reasonably necessary.

In the current app flow, deleting your account from the app is intended to remove your user document and the related favorites, check-ins, diary entries, and Firebase Authentication account.

7. Your Choices and Rights

  • You can choose whether to create an account or continue as a guest where available.
  • You can manage reminder settings inside the app and through your device notification settings.
  • You can choose whether to use Google, Apple, or Facebook login options that are enabled in your build.
  • You can delete diary entries, favorites, or other saved content from within the app where those controls are available.
  • You can request help with access, correction, or deletion through our contact channel.
If you cannot access the app and need help deleting your account or data, use the contact method in the Contact section below. For store compliance, make sure your public support page also explains how users can request deletion outside the app.

8. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards intended to protect information. These include account-based access controls and security features provided by our infrastructure partners. No system is perfectly secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children

Inspire Me Now is not directed to children under 13, or under the minimum digital age in the relevant jurisdiction, without parental or guardian involvement. If you believe a child has provided personal information in violation of applicable law, contact us so we can review and take appropriate action.

10. International Transfers

Our service providers may process or store information in countries other than the one where you live. By using the app, you understand that information may be transferred to and processed in these jurisdictions, subject to applicable safeguards and law.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect product changes, legal requirements, or operational updates. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date above. If a change is material, we may also provide additional notice inside the app or through store metadata where required.
